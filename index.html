Bio of Artist

Hi, I am Gontse J. Sithole, and I come from a suburban area called Lotus Gardens in the city of Pretoria.

I studied a BA in Fine Arts at the University of the Witwatersrand in Johannesburg, where I completed my degree and was awarded the Anya Millman Scholarship for outstanding practical work. I am also well-acquainted with the skill of photography, which I have practiced for 5 years.

My goal with this website is to have a discourse on the idea of change and its various stages, and perhaps to further demonstrate my skills as an artist.
